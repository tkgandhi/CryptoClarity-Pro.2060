About Us


The CryptoClarity Team

The developer of this website is Twisha Gandhi. She is in 9th grade at Parkland High School. She wanted to build this website because she loves programming and learning about technology, so what better way than to build a website about a current topic (cryptocurrency)! She hopes this website will prove her knowledge/skills in web-development and teach users about cryptocurrency.

How the Website was Built:

This website was created from scratch. It's made on the web-development platform Visual Studio Code using the languages HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have included the many features below on all of the pages to enhance the user experience on my website.

CryptoClarity (Home): 2 Marquee (track) elements (one with constantly updating coin prices).
Introduction Page: A progress bar on the top, text-to-speech buttons, and a back-to-top button.
Purpose Page: A fade-in image and labels, and a sliding feature.
DLTs Page: A step-by-step diagram and dropdowns.
History Page: A step-by-step timeline and a back-to-top button.
Types Page: The coin prices constantly update and an information panel opens after clicking on a coin type.
Risks Page: A video (multimedia element) that automatically starts playing in 10 seconds.
Quiz Page: A quiz with radio buttons and a system pop-up that displays the score when submitted.
Glossary Page: Filters the terms based on the category of the term.
Sign In Page: 2 forms taking user-input with field validation.
