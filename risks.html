Risks of Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are highly volatile and unregulated, risking total financial loss from sudden market crashes, cyber thefts, or lost access keys.

- Prices are very volatile and can rise or fall quickly.

- There is a high risk of scams, fraud, and fake projects.

- Hacking or security breaches can lead to permanent loss of funds.

- Losing access to private keys usually means losing your money forever.

"The risks of crypto" by 11Alive

Eliminating the Risks

- Research cryptocurrencies carefully before investing and avoid offers that seem too good to be true.

- Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and store funds in secure wallets.

- Only invest money you can afford to lose due to market volatility.

- Keep private keys and recovery phrases safe and never share them with anyone.
